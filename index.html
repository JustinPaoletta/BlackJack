• RULES

  • The Goal is To Get The Closest To 21 Without Going Over

  • Click The Deck To Deal A New Hand

  • Click Hit To Draw Another Card / Stay When Done Drawing

  • Click 'Dealers Turn' Button Until Their Turn Finishes

  • The Dealer Will Not Draw Another Card If They Have 17 or More

  • If You Get To 5 Cards And Havent Busted or You Get 21 points You Automatically Win

  • Aces are 11 unless you go over. Then They Will Switch To 1

  • You Can Cheat and View The Dealers Score In The Console (for testing purposes of course)

  • Finally.. to view your facedown card... hover over it
